The Internet is a network of networks

  • Sending systems segment the data, add a header and send the packets to the end system passing through hubs, switches and routers.
  • The sequence of communication links and packets traversed is a route or path.
  • End systems access the internet through ISP's.
  • The internet is a network of networks connected by routers.
